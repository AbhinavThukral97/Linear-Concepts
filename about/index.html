Our Background

Linear Concepts is an Architectural, Interior and Urban-Designing firm with a reputation for challenging convention and pursuing design excellence. At Linear Concepts we work in an intimate, responsive environment that collectively values good design, simplicity and positive relationships. We work closely with our clients to create an optimal end result that sets new standards, stands the test of time and is recognized as one of the best in the country.

We have worked on over 350 projects pan India since 1988 covering a wide range of sectors such as High Rise Housing, Retail, Corporate Interiors, Housing Development, High-End Villas, Hotels, Resorts among many others in Delhi, Gurgaon, Mumbai, Pushkar, Siliguri, Bhiwadi, Nagpur, Chennai, Pune in India and Dhaka, Copenhagen internationally.

We offer full architectural, urban designing & interior designing services for a wide range of projects - from houses & furniture to complex buildings and urban design. Our work is inspired by a singular purpose: 'To capture the spirit of client along with the essence of space.'

Linear Concepts wide portfolio is proud to be associated with Pareena Infrastructures, Ramprastha Group, RIIS Retail Denmark, Bestseller Retail India Pvt. Ltd, Bombay Rayons and have also delivered projects in prestigious projects like DLF Magnolias and some are ongoing in DLF Camellias, DLF The Crest, DLF 5, Sushant Lok-1, South City, DLF Phase 1,2,4 in Gurgaon & Turner Road, Bandra in Mumbai, Harley Davidson Showroom in Jaipur to name a few.
