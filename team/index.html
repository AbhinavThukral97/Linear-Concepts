Our Team

Sanjay Jethi
Principal Architect

Graduated from L.S Raheja College of Architecture with Honours in Bachelors in Architecture, Architect Sanjay Jethi is the Founder of Linear Concepts, one of the leading Architecture and Urban Design practices with a reputation for challenging convention and pursuing design excellence based out of New Delhi, India.

He has extensive professional experience in architecture and interior designing.He has worked on over 200 projects pan India covering a wide range of sectors such as High Rise Housing, Retail, Corporate Interiors, Housing Development, High-End Villas, Hotels, Resorts among many others. His major clients are Pareena Infrastructures Ltd, Ramprastha Builders, Cosmos Infra Engineering (India) Pvt. Ltd, and many other esteemed clients.

Shaleen Jethi
Architect, Partner

As an Architect and designer, Shaleen is interested in developing spatial tools that structure complex urban development strategies. Shaleen Jethi, a qualified Architect and urbanist was trained at Architectural Association, School of Architecture (AA), London where he pursued his M.Arch in Housing & Urbanism and completed his Bachelors in Architecture from KRVIA (University of Mumbai) in first class. Shaleen is a committed advocate of urban design and has worked on the development of the Yamuna riverfront as a part of his master’s thesis project.

Shaleen takes care of design, planning and production of a project from start to finish, focusing his imagination, vision and energy to achieve the ultimate. Shaleen’s innovative designs and builds are based on existing knowledge and in-depth research of the latest material developments and construction techniques.
