About Us

Linear Concepts is an Architectural, Interior and Urban-Designing firm with a reputation for challenging convention and pursuing design excellence. At Linear Concepts we work in an intimate, responsive environment that collectively values good design, simplicity and positive relationships. We work closely with our clients to create an optimal end result that sets new standards, stands the test of time and is recognized as one of the best in the country.

Our Work

We at Linear Concepts are not restricted by a set designing style or a box-ticking methodology. Instead, each project that we take up is a result of meticulous and in-depth designing process that involves understanding the needs and aspirations of our clients and providing them with innovative and design-led solutions tailored to them individually.

In collaboration with an extensive network of experts from varied fields and disciplines, we offer exceptional body of knowledge and expertise that embraces every aspect of a project's design, construction and performance.
